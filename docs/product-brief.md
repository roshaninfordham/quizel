# Product Brief

## Name

QuizRush Live

## One-Liner

A 25-second real-time quiz tournament from one QR code.

## Memory Sentence

The whole room scanned one QR code and became a live tournament bracket in 25 seconds.

## Problem

Live events, classrooms, sponsor booths, and team trainings struggle to make the whole audience actively participate. Most tools are passive polls, slow Q&A, or ordinary quiz screens.

## Solution

QuizRush Live turns the room into a synchronized multiplayer race. Everyone joins instantly from a phone, votes on topics, answers the same five fast questions, and watches the projector leaderboard/bracket move live.

## Technical Claim

Every join, topic vote, answer, score, rank jump, and replay event is reducer-owned state. The projector and phones subscribe to authoritative state instead of trusting client-local calculations.

## Why AI

AI routes crowd topic intent, generates the question pack, reviews fairness/safety, and summarizes the match. It is a live content pipeline with validation and fallback.
