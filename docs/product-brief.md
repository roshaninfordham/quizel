# Product Brief

QuizDuel Live turns a quiz into a room-scale realtime event.

Problem: most quiz apps make one person play while everyone else watches. In a hackathon demo room, that wastes the audience.

Solution: two Champions battle on the projector while everyone else joins the Crowd from their phones. The Crowd plays along, cheers with non-redeemable Energy, and climbs a Trust XP leaderboard.

Audience:

- Hackathon judges
- Sponsor engineers evaluating realtime state
- Event audiences who want to participate fast

Core promise:

- One QR code brings the whole room into the match.
- Every answer, Cheer, score, and leaderboard update is reducer-owned and live.
- AI agents generate, review, host, and recap the quiz without directly mutating scores.

Guardrail:

QuizDuel Live uses non-redeemable educational game XP only. There is no purchase, cash prize, withdrawal, transfer, or real-world value.
